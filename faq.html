What is "depth work"?

It's therapy that goes beneath the presenting problem. Rather than managing a symptom, we follow it to the pattern underneath: the old story, the unmet need, the belief quietly shaping how you meet the world, so that change can happen at the root rather than the surface.

What about retreats and psychedelic therapy?

Psychedelic therapy is a carefully held process of preparation, experience, and integration. It always begins with a one-on-one preparation session where we explore your intentions, health history, personal background, and what draws you to this work. Together, we assess whether it feels safe, supportive, and right for you at this time.

Preparation may include intention work, therapeutic exercises, nervous system regulation practices, and conversations to help you feel grounded, clear, and ready.

The retreat experience can be held in a small group setting or, if you feel called to a more personal container, in a one-on-one setting. I offer both options to support what feels best and most aligned for you.

Afterwards, integration therapy helps you make sense of what emerged and embody the insights in your everyday life.

This work is deeply individual and always approached with care, respect, and honesty. We take each step together. ♡

Could the deeper work be unsuitable for me?

Possibly, and that is why we begin with a conversation.

Together, we explore what is happening in your life right now, what is moving within you, and what is calling you towards deeper work. We take time to understand your emotional landscape, your intentions, your personal history, and where you currently are on your journey.

We also consider whether this feels like the right time for you. Are you in a place where you can welcome the insights that may arise? Do you feel ready to meet yourself more deeply and engage with the changes that this work can invite?

Your physical health is equally important. We carefully review your health history and current circumstances to ensure that your body and nervous system are in a position to safely support this process.

There is never any pressure to move forward. Sometimes the answer is yes, sometimes it is not yet. Together, we listen carefully and honestly to what feels most supportive for you.

The intention is never simply to have an experience, but to create the foundation for meaningful and lasting transformation when the timing, the circumstances, and your readiness are aligned.

What's your cancellation policy?

Life happens, and sometimes plans need to change.

For one-to-one sessions, I ask for 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Sessions cancelled within 24 hours are charged in full.

Retreat bookings are non-refundable, but your place may be moved to another retreat date if needed and if space is available.

If something unexpected comes up, please let me know as early as possible, and we'll find the best way forward together.
