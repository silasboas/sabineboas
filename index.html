The Work

Depth work — meeting what lives beneath the surface.

Therapy here is unhurried and inward. We move past the symptom toward the pattern underneath it.

Sessions are held one-to-one in both Copenhagen and Ibiza. The aim is not to erase what was loved, but to make space for what can come — to let grief soften, and new things grow.

For those drawn to deeper, longer-form processes, some retreats include psychedelic-assisted work — where it is legally available and clinically appropriate. A little about what that means is below.

A little about the medicine

Psilocybin?

A naturally occurring compound that, in a prepared and supported setting, gently loosens the mind's defences — making room for feeling and change.

5-HT2A agonism

Stimulates serotonin 2A receptors, shifting perception.1

Quieter default mode

Loosens self-referential thought, easing rumination.2

Neuroplasticity

Supports new neural connections (e.g. BDNF).3

Processing

With therapy, opens insight and breakthrough.

General neuroscience, shared for context — not medical advice or a treatment claim. Sources: Adebo et al., Fundamental & Clinical Pharmacology (2025); Carhart-Harris et al., PNAS (2012); Moliner et al., Nature Neuroscience (2023). Any psychedelic-assisted work is offered only where it is legally available and clinically appropriate.
